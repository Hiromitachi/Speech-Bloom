UPDATED EXERCISE FLOW:

1. Abdominal Breathing — 30-30-30
Instruction:
Place one hand on chest and one on abdomen.
Breathe using your abdomen.

Timer flow:
30 sec inhale
30 sec hold
30 sec exhale

Rounds: 1

2. Effortful Swallow
Instruction:
Swallow hard like swallowing a large bite.
Repetitions: 6
Use counter 0/6.

3. Masako Maneuver
Instruction:
Hold tongue gently between teeth and swallow.
Repetitions: 6
Use counter 0/6.

4. Tongue Side-to-Side
Instruction:
Move tongue left and right.
Repetitions: 6
Use counter 0/6.

5. Tongue Up and Down
Instruction:
Move tongue up and down.
Repetitions: 6
Use counter 0/6.

6. Tongue Round Movement
Instruction:
Move tongue in a circle around lips.
Repetitions: 6
Use counter 0/6.

7. Tongue Resistance
Instruction:
Press tongue against resistance.
Repetitions: 6
Use counter 0/6.

8. Cheek Puff Breathing
Instruction:
Take air into your mouth, puff both cheeks, hold, then slowly release.

Timer flow:
10 sec inhale
10 sec hold cheeks puffed
10 sec exhale/release

Repetitions: 6

9. Cheek Side-to-Side
Instruction:
Move air from left cheek to right cheek.
Repetitions: 6
Use counter 0/6.

10. Say A I U
Instruction:
Take a deep breath, then say A, I, U slowly and clearly while exhaling.

Timer flow per repetition:
5 sec inhale
10 sec exhale/say A-I-U

Repetitions: 6
